Adobe Commerce Getting Started Guide

https://experienceleague.adobe.com/docs/commerce-admin/start/guide-overview.html?lang=en

This guide is intended for merchants and system administrators that are new to Adobe Commerce and Magento Open Source. It provides an overview of the platform from their perspective and some detailed information about basic features that enable a functional store.
本指南适用于对Adobe Commerce和Magento Open Source不熟悉的商家和系统管理员。它从他们的角度提供了平台的概述，并提供了一些关于基本功能的详细信息，以实现一个功能齐全的商店。

This guide covers:

SubjectDescription
Release InformationLearn about the current Adobe Commerce and Magento Open Source release.
了解当前的Adobe Commerce和Magento开源版本发布。
Login Credentials and URLsLearn about the Commerce URLs and account credentials used to gain access to your Admin and to your storefront.
了解用于访问您的管理后台和商店前台的商务URL和帐户凭据。
Commerce accountsLearn about Commerce accounts, which manage the products and services that you have purchased.
了解商务账户，管理您购买的产品和服务。
The AdminLearn about the Commerce Admin, the place where merchants set up products and promotions, manage orders, and perform other administrative tasks.
了解商务管理，这是商家设置产品和促销活动、管理订单以及执行其他管理任务的地方。
The StorefrontLearn about the pages and functional elements that your store can provide to support the shopping experience for your customers.
了解您的商店可以提供的页面和功能元素，以支持客户的购物体验。
Basic Setup and ConfigurationLearn the concepts of store hierarchy and configuration scope and walk through the basic configuration settings.
学习店铺层次结构和配置范围的概念，并浏览基本配置设置。
Site ComplianceLearn how merchants are responsible to meet industry guidelines for maintaining a secure environment, and to meet the legal requirements and best practices for online merchants in their jurisdiction.
了解商家如何负责遵守行业准则，以维护安全环境，并满足其所在地区在线商家的法律要求和最佳实践。
Standard ReportingLearn how to filter data and generate online reports. The report data can be opened in a spreadsheet or imported into other applications.
学习如何筛选数据并生成在线报告。报告数据可以在电子表格中打开或导入到其他应用程序中。
ResourcesLearn about the wealth of business and technical resources, self-help tools, and services that help you succeed.
了解丰富的商业和技术资源、自助工具和服务，助您取得成功。

It does not cover the more detailed capabilities of Adobe Commerce and Magento Open Source.
它不涵盖Adobe Commerce和Magento Open Source的更详细功能。
